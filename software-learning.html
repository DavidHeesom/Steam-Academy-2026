STEAM Academy 2026 — University of Wolverhampton

Software Learning

A curated set of video playlists to help you get to grips with Autodesk Revit — the primary BIM authoring tool used throughout the programme. Work through these at your own pace, before or during the Summer School.

These playlists have been created by Prof David Heesom specifically to support students on the STEAM Academy programme. Whether you are completely new to Revit or have some prior experience, working through these videos will give you a solid foundation before the hands-on sessions begin. Each playlist builds progressively — start with Playlist 1 if you are a beginner.

01

Introduction to Autodesk Revit
Interface & Basic Modelling

Start here if you are new to Revit. This playlist walks you through the interface, navigation, and the fundamentals of creating your first architectural model — covering walls, floors, roofs, doors and windows.
