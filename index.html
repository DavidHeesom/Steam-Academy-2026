About the Programme

Not an Overview.
The Real Thing.

The STEAM Academy 2026 is a ten-day, hands-on programme designed for international students who want more than a theoretical introduction to digital construction.

From day one you will be working in Autodesk Revit, building intelligent BIM models to ISO 19650 international standards. You will simulate 4D construction sequences, carry out clash detection, and create photorealistic Virtual Reality environments using TwinMotion — then walk through buildings you have designed yourself.

You will work with real laser scanning equipment, capturing physical spaces and converting them into precise digital models. Every session is structured so that you leave the room having achieved something, not merely observed something.

Programme Highlights

Autodesk Revit BIM Modelling — data-rich models to ISO 19650 standards from day one

4D Construction Simulation — sequencing, clash detection and cost scheduling

Virtual Reality with TwinMotion — photorealistic walkthrough of your own designs

Laser Scanning & Point Clouds — reality capture converted to live BIM models

What You Will Learn

Industry Skills.
Real Software.

The tools and workflows covered in this programme are what employers across every major construction market are demanding right now.

BIM Fundamentals

Build intelligent, data-rich models in Autodesk Revit conforming to ISO 19650 — the global standard governing information management across the building lifecycle.

4D Simulation

Link your BIM model to construction schedules. Visualise sequences, identify clashes, and understand cost implications before a single component is built.

Virtual Reality

Create photorealistic environments in TwinMotion and experience your own designs at full scale in VR — a skill increasingly expected in design and client-facing roles.

Reality Capture

Use professional laser scanning equipment to capture real buildings and convert the resulting point clouds into precise, useable digital models.
